Data Reduction for Chopper Spectrometers

Data Reduction for Chopper Spectrometers (DRCS or DrChops) is an open-source software to reduce data from direct-geometry time-of-flight neutron chopper spectrometers. It is a product of the DANSE project.

Reliable

As an example, the figure on the right contains two DOS curves reduced with Dr Chops for VPt at room temperature, measured at two different neutron chopper spectrometers, LRMECS and Pharos, respectively. The agreement between two curves is amazingly good, and that shows the reliability of the reduction procedure.

Easy to use

DrChops provides simple GUIs for some reduction applications. Those GUIs basically present parameters needed for reduction in a hierarchial way and let users to navigate through those configurable items and configure them.

Fully extensible
